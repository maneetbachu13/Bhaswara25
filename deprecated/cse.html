BALL RUSH

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Block-1,2nd Floor,Room No. 221

*Our event consists of 3 rounds*

    Round 1: BUILD AND BREAK

    Round 2: QUCIK SEIZE

    Round 3:THREE BEE

Note: A Team should consist of Two Members

Rules and Regulations

  • Round 1: One person of the team should build a tower (on a table) with glasses (of 5 levels) and the other person is given 3 chances to hit the tower

    Out of 20 teams, 12 teams will be qualified to the next round

  • Round 2: One person in the team should hit the ground with the ball and the other should catch it with a glass and place a glass on that glass and so on… Time Limit(60 seconds)

    Out of 12 teams,6 teams are qualified to the final round

  • Round 3: One person in the team should blow the ball from a glass(filled with water) into the final glass(which is empty) and the task of the other person is to move the glass placed on the table to the other side of the table with the help of balloon's air

    Here, the winner is decided based on the duration team takes to complete the round!

CODE FIESTA

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

This event consists of 3 rounds

    Round 1: Picnary

    Round 2: Comprocon

    Round 3: 404 not found

Rules and Regulations

    Round 1

    • Team division (max 3)
    • Each team will be given a question paper
    • From the given paper, 5 questions will be asked
    • After finishing 1st round 8 teams will be qualified to 2nd round

    Round 2

    • The team will be divided into 1 computer and 2 programmers
    • The computer will give 3 tasks to the programmers
    • The programmers should perform the tasks given by the computer
    • The task can be performed either in the same or opposite manner
    • After finishing 2nd round 5 teams will be qualified to 3rd round

    Round 3

    • This is an individual round
    • Each participant will be given 3 code snippets

Scoring

  • For every correct answer team will get 1 point
  • For 1st wrong answer, 1 point will be deducted and they will be given another chance
  • If the teams misuse the chance or unable to answer, 1 point will be deducted from total team score

ROBOT RUSH

DARE TO RACE AND WIN TO CHERISH

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Block-5,Back Side Open Yard

Rules for the Race: In this competition the contestant or team of contestants design a robot wireless within the specified dimensions that can operated manually. The robot that will complete the specified task in least time will be the winner.

Robot Specifications
  • Vehicle Requirements: The robots may be either fully autonomous or remotely controlled. If remotely controlled, the robots must be operated by a human operator who is not physically on the course. The vehicles must be electric and have a maximum 20 cm Height and 25 cm width size limit. The exact specifications may vary depending on the competition
  • Track: The track surface and course line may have unevenness. There will be certain obstacles in the race track which will try to slow down the robot
  • Race Format: The race may have several rounds, each consisting of a certain number of laps. The fastest vehicle to complete the set number of laps in the shortest time is declared the winner
  • Safety: Safety is a top priority during the race, and there are strict safety rules in place to ensure that the vehicles do not cause any harm to spectators or other competitors. The vehicles must pass various safety tests and have safety features installed to participate in the race
  • Technical Inspection: All participating vehicles must undergo technical inspection before the race to ensure that they comply with the rules and specifications. Any vehicle that fails the technical inspection will not be allowed to participate in the race

Each team should consist of 2 members

Our event consists of 2 Rounds

    Round 1

    • The participants will be given a field to race in which they have to show the agility and control over their robot
    • The field may consist of bumps and turns
    • The first 5 participants who reach the task will be qualified to round 2

    Round 2

    • In this the qualified players will be given obstacles to face
    • The obstacles may contain ramps, bridges, zig zag roads, slippery paths etc
    • The first 2 participants who complete this will be qualified for selection of the winner and runner

AIML EVENTS

BID BUY CODE

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Block-1 Room No-E05,E06,105,106

Bid buy code is a technical event conducted by ADROIT DAEMONS, club of AIML. In this event participants will be working together as a team (max of 5 or can come individually and teams will be allocated on the spot) to buy questions from a wide array of questions available to them. Each team will be allocated a certain number of initial credits. The questions will be sold to the team members through a process of auction. In the auction the teams need to compete against each other and try to buy the questions using the credits. Each question depending upon the difficulty will have a different base price. Any team can buy up to a max of 3 questions. After the auction is over the teams will then come up with a code for the question or questions which they bought. When stuck the teams can use the remaining credits to buy hints for themselves. The team which stands at the top of the leader board at the end of the coding and also has maximum number of credits left wins the EVENT.

The team which hunts the treasure may not be the winner but the team which gets the highest value of coins will win the game

CODE QUEST

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Block-5 E10,E11

Each Team should Consist 3 members

Event Description : In this event, the participants are required to use their basic programming knowledge to decode a series of clues in order to find the hidden treasure. The clues will be provided in the form of tiny code snippets and the participants will have to guess the output of the particular code snippet which will lead them to the next clue.

Our event consists of 3 Rounds

    Round 1

      In this round the participants will be divided into teams of 3 and they will have to find out their treasure box by guessing the output of the provided clue which is nothing but a code snippet. The clue will give them details about the color of the box or the shape of the box. Those teams that find the correct treasure box in the stipulated time will be qualified to the next round

    Elimination
      Those teams failing to find the treasure boxes and keys will be disqualified in round 1.

    Round 2

      In this round the qualified teams will have to find the key to unlock the treasure box. The keys are assigned unique natural numbers and the provided clues will help them identify the right key. Those teams that manages to unlock the treasure box in the stipulated time will be qualified to the next round.

    Elimination

      Those teams failing to find the treasure boxes and keys will be disqualified in round 2

    Round 3

      In this round the qualified teams will find a simple problem statement inside the treasure box and that team that comes up with the optimized solution first will win the event

RELAY ART

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Each Team should Consist 3 members

Event Description :In this event, the participants are expected to showcase their creativity and artistic skills. This is a team event which requires the team members to paint together and complete the drawing in turns within the stipulated time

Our event consists of 3 Rounds

    Round 1

      In this round the teams will be shown different pictures and after each image is shown the teams will be given a total of 90 seconds witch each member given 30 seconds each. After the 30 seconds have elapsed the one who is drawing will have to hand it over to the next member. Those teams whose drawing is the closest to the image will receive a point. And finally those teams that score more than the threshold will be qualified to the next round

    Elimination
      Those teams failing to clear the minimum threshold points will be disqualified in round 1

    Round 2

      In this round the qualified teams will be given names of certain things that we use in our daily lives and they are required to follow the same process as in round 1 and those teams that draw creatively in the stipulated time will get a point and finally those teams that score more than threshold will be qualified to the next round

    Elimination

      Those teams failing to clear the minimum threshold points will be disqualified in round 2

    Round 3

      In this round the qualified teams will be given a theme and a total time of 10 minutes, that team whose drawing justifies the theme well will be declared the winner.
