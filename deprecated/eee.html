    LOGICAL QUEST

    Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

    Venue: Block -1, G05 BEE LAB-1

    Timings: 11:30AM - 1:30 PM

    Total number of rounds - 3

    Rules and Regulations

    • ID cards are compulsory
    • Dress code should be formal.
    • Should not mess the otheí team ciícuits while they hunt.
    • Registrations are accepted till the date of the event.

    LEVEL1-RESISTOR FINDING

  • A team will be formed depending on the no of registrations min 2-3
  • 3 Random resistors and a breadboard will be given to each team
  • Team members together have to find out the value of the resistor and connect them in series/parallelOn the breadboard in the given time
  • The elimination of teams in level-1 depends on the no of participants

    • Level 2: THE MAZE GAME

  • The qualified team will be able to play the maze game Where participants individually or group of 2 have to complete the game
  • The maze path has a few numbers from 1 to 5 and each group will be given chits which have different resistor values for all 1 to 5 numbers and the type of sum will be given series/parallel
  • The group that finds the path and sum the values of the resistors in less time will get qualifies for level -3 (final level)

    • Round 3: RAPID QUIZ

  • The qualified groups or participants have to answer 10 questions and for each question, 10sec will be counted
  • Participants who get the highest scores 1st and 2nd prizes will be awarded
