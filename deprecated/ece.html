Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Block 1, Room Number – 121

Timings: 10:30AM - 3:30 PM

Total number of rounds - 3

  • Round 1: Mind-Bender
  • Round 2: The Eliminator
  • Round 3: Greek out

Rules and Regulations

  • Id Cards are Compulsory
  • Mobile phones are not allowed.
  • After completion of each round eliminated participants must leave without any argument.
  • All the participants must follow the instructions of the coordinators and volunteers. So that we can success the event.

Round 1: Mind-Bender

  • In this round, you will solve crossword puzzles where you will be given hints and you need to provide the answers. The highest number of correct answers will qualify you for the second round.

    • Round 2: The Eliminator

  • You will be given a sheet of codes, and you need to find and fix errors. The participant who finds the maximum number of errors will qualify for the third round.

    • Round 3: Greek out

  • You will be tested on your coding skills with three challenging questions that you need to answer within 45 minutes. There will be three prizes for completion.

    CIRCUIT CIRCUS

    Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

    Venue: Block 1, 1st floor, Room no, 118

    Timings: 10:30AM - 3:30 PM

    Total number of rounds - 3

    • Round 1: The lost notebook
    • Round 2: Two truths and a lie
    • Round 3: Beat drop!

    Rules and Regulations

    • The teams registered in the form are final. Students cannot be interchanged in the last minute
    • Mobile phones are not allowed.
    • Check the clues of your own team, if you find the clue of any other team, leave it in the same place where it was found
    • Any misbehavior with a co participant or coordinator, will not be tolerated. The team will be eliminated immediately.
    • The event should go in harmony. Hence, we expect all the student’s cooperation for the same
    • Components should not be damaged

    Round 1: The lost notebook

  • Teams are given clues to find out the components and build the circuit at the endusing them. The teams that complete the circuit and show its working will be qualified to the next round

    • Round 2: Two truths and a lie

  • Few set of statements regarding a component or a technology are given to the teams and they are expected to find out the lie among the similar looking statements. The 4 teams which answer maximum of them correctly are qualified to the next round.

    • Round 3: Beat drop!

  • This game is between 2 teams each time. One from each team has to come forward and snatch the object kept in between when the beat drops. The winning team gets to answer the question and gain a point.
