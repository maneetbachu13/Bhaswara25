IT Events

CODE FIESTA

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: E15

Team Event - 3 Members

In this event, the participants are required to brush up their basic technical knowledge to identify and programming languages to debug basic codes given. Each and every participant will get an fair chance to answer the questions and the winners will be decided according to the points.

Rules and Regulations

Round - 1 Picnary

In this round, each team is given five questions. Each question contains images, and the team needs to guess the technical word from it. Each team should guess at least three technical words.

Elimination

Team that fails to guess at least three technical words will be disqualified in round 1.

Round - 2 Comprocon

In this round, the qualified teams will have to divide into one computer and two programmers, the computer is given a set of three tasks and gives commands to it’s programmers in the form of the IF, IF_ELSE, IF_THEN_ELSE, and then programmers should perform those tasks according to the computer command’s.

Elimination

Teams that fail to perform at least two tasks will be disqualified in round 2.

Round - 3 404 not found

This is an individual round where each participant will be given four code snippets with errors in it. The codes will be in C, JAVA programming languages. The participant should find the error and he/she should debug it. The top two participants who debug all the four codes in a short period of +me will be declared as winners.

SQUAD24ESPORTS’23

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: E16

This event is designed for players who have honed their skills in the popular mobile game, Battlegrounds Mobile India. Players will have the opportunity to compete in a variety of game modes. Each match will be played on a different map, and players will need to use their skills and strategy to outlast their opponents

Rules and Regulations

Round - 1

In the first round the allowance of participants is up to 60 members. Each team consists of 4 participants. Here top 2 team will be selected and again their will be a Erangel match conducted between the top 2 finalists of 1st round and the and the team with highest points will be considered as the 1st finalist of round 1 and will be qualified to round 3 and the others teams will be disqualified.

Round - 2

In second round also their will be a total of 60 participants and top 2 teams will be selected for the Erangel match and a final team will be selected based on the points achieved by the teams and will be qualified to round 3. The other teams will be disqualified from the round

Round - 3

In the third round a TDM match will be conducted between the final teams selected from round 1 and round 2 and a final team will be selected based on the points and will be considered as the final winner.
