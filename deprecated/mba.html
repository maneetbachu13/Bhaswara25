Busy Quizy Den

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Block 3, Third Floor, Room 318

Timings: 11:30AM - 4:00 PM

Rules and Regulations

Total number of rounds - 3

Busy Quizy Den is a fun and educative way to test your knowledge related to business intern makes you a better businessman.

Total number of rounds - 3

  1. PPT (consists of taglines, Logos, CEO's name, Ambassador)
  2. Questions from Advertisements
  3. Cross puzzle

Round - 1

A question will be asked to a team and if they are unable to answer, it will be passed to the next team. Each team would be asked 4 questions.

  • 10 marks for correct answer and 5 negative marks for wrong answer.
  • If a team cannot answer the question, they can pass the question to the next team
  • Answering time is only 30 sec.
  • Team discussion is allowed

Round - 2 Buzzer Round

  • A question will be fired & the team who press the buzzer first to answer first
  • 10 marks for the correct answer and -5marks for a negative answer or if team doesn't answer after pressing the buzzer. (forwarding the question is not allowed in this round)
  • Answering time 30 sec

Round - 3

  • In this round each team have to answer questions by framing the answer with the given blocks.
  • Answering time 120 sec

General Rules

  • Each team may consist of a maximum of three.
  • The time limit for each team is 1 minute.
  • The registration fee for this event is 50/- per person.
  • ID CARDS ARE COMPULSORY.
  • Dress code should be formal.
  • Required stationary will be provided by the organizers affiliation certificate will be provided for each participant,
  • The Winning team will be rewarded with an attractive cash prize.
  • Judge decision will be final and will not be subjected to any further discussions.
  • Judgement is based on overall team performance.
