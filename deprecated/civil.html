A Minute to win it

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: Geology Lab-301,Block-III

Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Open to all branches.
  • ID cards are compulsory.
  • Complete the task in a given time.

Round - 1

  • Student have to pull out one block from the existing structure
  • Without disturbing consecutive blocks.
  • Only one member should play by using single hand.
  • Whoever will remove the block within the time will selected to next round.
  • The limit for this round is 30sec.

Round - 2

  • Students have to form a different type of structure related to civil ex:construction of building,dams etc
  • The limit for this round is 1 minute.

Elimination

In first round,who takes more than 30sec to complete task will be eliminated

More Details

  1. The registration fee for this event is Rs.50/- per person.
  2. Cash prize for the 1st and 2nd winners will be given.
  3. participation certificate and winners certification will be given.

TREASURE HUNT

Date of the Event: 21st March 2024

Venue: CAD lab-314, Block-III,3rd Floor

Rules and Regulations

General Rules

  • Open to all branches.
  • ID cards are compulsory.
  • Complete the task in a given time.

Round - 1

  • Participants will be informed to bring dupattas of specified colour, Instagram reel with specific rules and selfie with a person who has tattoo.
  • One who gets the above things will be shortlisted to the second round.
  • Time limit for this round is 30mins.

Round - 2

  • Participants are given a list of clues which leads them to a destination where an item is placed from which another clue is given which leads to a destination where an item is placed.
  • One who gets the item first will be shortlisted to the final round
  • Time limit for this round is 1 hour.

Round - 3

  • Participants have to decode the given code and have to bring the respective item.
  • One who gets the item first will be the winners.
  • TTime limit for this round is 30mins.
