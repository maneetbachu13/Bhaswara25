About Geethanjali

Geethanjali College of Engineering & Technology is dedicated to staying ahead of the curve in a rapidly evolving technological landscape.The vision of providing students with an unparalleled educational experience, one that caters to their holistic development and empowers them to become well-rounded global citizens.The future demands not only technical expertise but also a broader perspective. That's why we offer a unique blend of rigorous academics, practical application, and personal growth opportunities. Our integrated curriculum features a diverse range of technical courses, interdisciplinary research projects, industry collaborations, and workshops on entrepreneurship and personality development.Through this comprehensive approach, we equip our students with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in the globalized world.
